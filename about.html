the story

Our harpist has been providing services for wedding and corporate functions since 2009 in the Gauteng region of South Africa; playing multiple genres and styles of music; both solo and with accompaniment to suit the functions needs.


Vivienne is a classically trained harpist with orchestral experience. Educated at the prestigious Interlochen Center for the Arts; Vivienne has gained experience playing in orchestras and with musicians from around the world.
